# 🔬 Boltzmann Labs

### Enabling Autonomous Scientific Discoveries

Boltzmann Labs is an AI-driven scientific discovery company focused on accelerating innovation across biotechnology, drug discovery, computational biology, and life sciences.

We build AI systems that help researchers analyze complex scientific data, generate insights, and support decision-making across scientific workflows. Our work combines machine learning, deep learning, computational chemistry, bioinformatics, and autonomous AI systems to address challenges in scientific research and development.

🌐 Website: https://boltzmann.co

---

## Our Mission

Our mission is to accelerate scientific discovery through advanced artificial intelligence, enabling researchers and organizations to solve complex problems more efficiently and effectively.

By combining scientific expertise with modern AI techniques, we aim to create tools and platforms that support innovation in healthcare, biotechnology, chemistry, and related scientific domains.

## Open Science & Open Source

Boltzmann Labs believes that scientific progress is accelerated through collaboration, transparency, and reproducibility.

Through our Hugging Face organization, we aim to share:

* Machine learning models for scientific applications
* Curated datasets and benchmarks
* Research artifacts and evaluation resources
* Open-source tools and workflows
* Educational resources for the scientific AI community

We support responsible and reproducible AI research and welcome contributions from researchers, developers, and organizations worldwide.
