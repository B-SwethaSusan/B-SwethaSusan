## About

**Boltzmann Labs** is an AI-powered scientific discovery company founded in **2019**, building the next generation of autonomous research platforms. We develop multi-agent AI systems that accelerate innovation across drug discovery, biotechnology, chemistry, materials science, precision medicine, and life sciences.

Our platform bridges the gap between raw scientific data and actionable discoveries — enabling researchers, pharmaceutical companies, biotechs, and academic institutions to move faster, with greater precision and confidence.

> *"We are building AI systems that don't just assist scientists — they reason, hypothesize, and discover alongside them."*

---

## Mission & Vision

**Mission** — Transform conventional scientific research and drug discovery through AI, machine learning, deep learning, and autonomous agent-based systems, dramatically compressing the timeline from hypothesis to validated discovery.

**Vision** — Become the platform powering world-changing scientific ventures through autonomous, intelligent, and collaborative AI systems that operate at the frontier of human knowledge.

## Open Source Commitment

We are strong advocates for open science and reproducible research. The future of scientific AI depends on collaborative, transparent, and accessible tools.

Our contributions to the community include pre-trained scientific foundation models, curated annotated datasets for benchmarking and training, reusable scientific workflow components, and documented reproducible research protocols. We actively contribute to the broader scientific AI ecosystem and encourage the community to build upon our work.
